**What is Gibberish?**

At its core, gibberish is language – language that *looks* like language, but doesn't quite *do* anything. It’s constructed to resemble real language, often with grammatically incorrect or nonsensical phrases, but it doesn’t convey any meaningful information. It’s a playful, experimental form of communication.
