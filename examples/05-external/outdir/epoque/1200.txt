Hark, good sir! 'Tis but a short while ago, in the year of our Lord 1204, that the Fourth Crusade, which was meant to reclaim the Holy Land, instead turned its wrath upon the great Christian city of Constantinople. A most lamentable and bewildering turn of events, indeed! The city was sacked, and the Byzantine Empire, a bulwark against the infidel, was grievously wounded. A dark day for Christendom, I say!
