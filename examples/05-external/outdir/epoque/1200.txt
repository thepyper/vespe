Hark, good sir! A grand happening indeed, and but a few years past! In the year of our Lord 1192, the noble King Richard, Lionheart of England, did sign a truce with the great Saladin, Sultan of Egypt and Syria, ending the Third Crusade. 'Twas a mighty struggle, and many a brave knight did fall, but peace, for a time, hath returned to the Holy Land.
