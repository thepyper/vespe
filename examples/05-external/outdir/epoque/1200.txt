Certes, good sir! A grand event hath recently transpired, one that shall surely echo through the ages. In the year of our Lord 1204, the Fourth Crusade, instead of marching upon the Holy Land as was intended, turned its might upon the great city of Constantinople, the very heart of the Eastern Roman Empire! 'Twas a sight to behold, though a sorrowful one for many, as the city was sacked and its treasures scattered. A new Latin Empire hath been established there, though many question its legitimacy. Such are the shifting tides of power in these turbulent times!
