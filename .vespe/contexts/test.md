**3. Providing a Simple Coconut and Jelly Recipe (Focusing on a Classic):**

**Simple Coconut and Jelly Delight**

**Yields:** 4-6 servings
**Prep time:** 15 minutes
**Chill time:** 30 minutes (minimum)


**Ingredients:**

*   1 cup Fresh Coconut Milk (full-fat is best for a richer flavor)
*   1 cup Fruit Jelly (cherry, strawberry, or grape are all good choices – you can buy a ready-made one or make your own using fruit juice and pectin)
*   1-2 tablespoons Lemon Juice (to brighten the flavors)
*   Optional: Pinch of Cinnamon or Star Anise for a warm spice

**Instructions:**

1.  **Combine:** In a saucepan, combine the coconut milk and fruit jelly.
2.  **Simmer:** Heat over medium heat, stirring gently, until the jelly is heated through and the coconut milk is slightly warmed (about 5-7 minutes). *Do not boil!*
3.  **Flavor:** Stir in the lemon juice and cinnamon/star anise if using.
4.  **Chill:** Pour the mixture into small ramekins or glasses (about 4-6 ounces per serving). Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
5.  **Serve:** Serve chilled.


**Tips for Success:**

*   **Coconut Milk is Key:** Use full-fat coconut milk for the best flavor and texture.
*   **Don’t Overheat:** Overheating can cause the jelly to separate.
*   **Adjust Sweetness:** Taste and adjust the lemon juice as needed.
